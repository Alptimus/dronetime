Drone Flight & Recharge Time Calculator

Flight Time Calculation

Step 1: Convert Capacity
Convert battery capacity from milliampere-hours (mAh) to ampere-hours (Ah):
Capacity (Ah) = Capacity (mAh) / 1000

Step 2: Calculate Energy
Determine the energy stored in the battery in watt-hours (Wh):
Energy (Wh) = Voltage (V) × Capacity (Ah)

Step 3: Compute Flight Time
Estimate the flight time by dividing the energy by the average power draw (in watts):
Flight Time (hours) = Energy (Wh) / Power Draw (W)
Convert the result to minutes by multiplying by 60.

Recharge Time Calculation

Step 1: Ideal Recharge Time
Calculate the ideal recharge time (in hours) by dividing the battery capacity (Ah) by the charger current (A):
Ideal Recharge Time (hours) = Capacity (Ah) / Charger Current (A)

Step 2: Adjust for Real-World Conditions
Multiply the ideal time by an efficiency factor (typically 1.2) to account for the constant-current and constant-voltage phases, as well as cell balancing:
Recharge Time (hours) = (Capacity (Ah) / Charger Current (A)) × Efficiency Factor
Then, convert the result to minutes by multiplying by 60.

These formulas help you estimate how long your drone can fly based on your battery's energy and how long it will take to recharge using your specified charger parameters.
